JAPANESE CURRY

Description

This is japanese curry. It is pretty good and made with beef, curry, and carrots

Ingredients

  • 1 lb Beef cubes
  • 3 Carrots
  • 1/2 an Onion
  • 2 Curry cubes
  • 1 Scallion
  • 1/4 cup of Milk
  • 2 cups of rice

Steps

  1. Step 1: Chop up the carrots and marinate the beef
  2. Step 2: Cook the beef cubes and then set aside
  3. Step 3: Cook the carrots and add 2 and 1/2 cups of water
  4. Step 4: Bring the carrots to boil and then simmer for 10 minutes
  5. Step 5: After simmering, add in the beef and the two curry cubes
  6. Step 6: Mix everything. Then add in the milk and stir
  7. Step 7: Finally, wait until it thickens and then turn off the heat. Add in the scallions
  8. Step 8: Finish and serve!
